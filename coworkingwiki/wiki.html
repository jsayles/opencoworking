Coworking is growing rapidly around the globe, but our ability to connect with all these awesome people is lagging behind. One of our primary tools for collaboration, the Coworking Wiki, is not keeping up. We want to turn the wiki into a platform that people like using, that can grow as coworking grows, and that puts our best face forward to the world.

Be a part of it! We're asking for your support.

Donate to vastly improve the coworking wiki. Help our best tool for global collaboration catch up with the movement's growth. Read all about the vision, the plan, and how to donate!
