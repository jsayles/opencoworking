The Vision

“It’s no surprise that the wiki was built from the ground up by avid contributors to the open source movement. They laid the ground work for thousands of people to share what they have learned, because they believe in the greater value that stems from open collaboration.” -- Will Bennis, Locus Workspace

Jacob Sayles and Will Bennis have maintained the wiki for a few years, but as volunteers, aren’t able to keep up with the growth in the movement.

Help us build the movement together.

The improved wiki will be an effective platform to:

  • Discover: We’ll have maps!

  • Collaborate: We want to share our experiences in a friendly and accessible way.

  • Educate: When someone like your mom goes to the wiki, you want her to be able to easily understand it all and maybe ask questions.
