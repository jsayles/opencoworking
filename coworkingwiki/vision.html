The Vision

“It’s no surprise that the wiki was built from the ground up by avid contributors to the open source movement. They laid the groundwork for thousands of people to share what they have learned, because they believe in the greater value that stems from open collaboration.”
-- Will Bennis, Locus Workspace

Jacob Sayles and Will Bennis have maintained the coworking wiki for a few years, and as volunteers, only have time to prevent spam and approve new users. This system isn't able to keep up with the growth of the movement and we believe we can do so much more.

Help us build the movement together.

The improved wiki will be an effective platform to:

  • Discover: We’ll have maps!

  • Collaborate: We want to share our experiences in a friendly and accessible way.

  • Educate: When someone new comes to the wiki, you want her to be able to easily understand it all and ask questions.
"The wiki helps with our SEO, sends us hot leads for membership inquiries and endless drop ins. From the 80-100 referrals we get per month, we easily save $250-$300 per month in CPC fees. Now imagine when it’s working optimally."
-- Toby Morning, Citizen Space
