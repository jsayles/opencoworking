Open Coworking Trust

     In 2012 I founded this non-profit as a quiet, behind-the-scenes, entity to help me do all the things I was already doing. I've been passionate and involved in the global coworking movement since I learned about it in early 2007. I needed a way to support the larger efforts I am involved in like the coworking wiki upgrade project I'm launching at the coworking conference in Austin on March 8th, 2012. There isn't much to see here, and that is by design. If you are interested in talking with me more about this or any of my many coworking ambitions feel free to drop me a line, or you can find me at Office Nomads most days.
