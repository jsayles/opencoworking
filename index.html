Open Coworking

Mission: Promote and facilitate greater collaboration between coworking spaces around the world by supporting projects that demonstrate and emphasize the core coworking values of collaboration, openness, community, accessibility, and sustainability.

History: In 2012 I founded this non-profit as a quiet, behind-the-scenes, entity to help me do all the things I was already doing. I've been passionate and involved in the global coworking movement since I learned about it in early 2007 while starting Office Nomads. I co-founded Coworking Seattle in 2009 and I'm launching a project to upgrade the coworking wiki in Austin on March 8th, 2012.
